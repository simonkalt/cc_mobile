sAImon Software ("we," "us," "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the personal information you provide when you:

This policy applies to all users, visitors, customers, and subscribers ("you," "your").

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

Contact Information

  • Phone number (including SMS opt-in number)

Usage Data

  • Information related to your interaction with our messaging program and website.

Opt-In / Consent Data

3. How We Use Personal Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Deliver SMS Messages: To send you the SMS alerts, notifications, promotions, or updates that you explicitly opted in to receive.
  • Service Administration: To manage and improve our messaging services and customer support.
  • Compliance and Legal: To comply with applicable laws, guidelines, and carrier requirements.

When you provide your phone number and consent to receive SMS messages, you are explicitly authorizing us to send messages to that number. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Standard message carrier fees from your wireless provider may apply.

4. SMS Opt-In, Opt-Out, and Help Instructions

  • Opt-In: You will only receive SMS messages if you have affirmatively opted in using a clearly labeled mechanism on our website (such as a checkbox on our contact form) or by texting a specific keyword. The opt-in language discloses the nature of the messaging program, that message frequency varies, and that message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-Out: You may discontinue receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After texting STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message.
  • Help: For assistance, reply HELP to any message or email support@saimonsoft.com.
